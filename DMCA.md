# DMCA Policy & Takedown Requests

## About Koware

Koware is an **open-source software tool** that functions as a search aggregator and media player launcher. It is designed to be a general-purpose utility that:

- Does **not** host, store, or distribute any media content
- Does **not** include any pre-configured sources or links to streaming services
- Requires users to manually configure their own source definitions
- Acts purely as a client-side search interface

## No Bundled Sources

**Koware ships with zero pre-configured sources.** All source configurations must be provided by the user. The software itself contains no URLs, API endpoints, or references to any third-party streaming services.

Users of Koware are solely responsible for:

1. Ensuring they have the legal right to access any sources they configure
2. Complying with applicable laws in their jurisdiction
3. Respecting the terms of service of any services they access
4. Obtaining proper licenses for any content they access

## What We Cannot Remove

Since Koware does not include, host, or link to any streaming content, we cannot:

- Remove links to streaming sites (we don't include any)
- Block access to specific content (we don't control what users configure)
- Take down user-configured sources (these exist only on users' local machines)

## Response to Valid Requests

For valid DMCA requests regarding code in this repository, we will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Review the claim
3. Take appropriate action, which may include removing or disabling access to the allegedly infringing code
4. Notify any affected parties as required by law

## Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification including:

1. Your physical or electronic signature
2. Identification of the removed material and its previous location
3. A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake
4. Your name, address, phone number, and consent to jurisdiction
5. A statement that you will accept service of process
